A web app for new students to the department. 
They can use the app to understand the sequence of classes required to complete their major/minor in this department, depending on career interests (game design, web, databases, devops, etc). With potential features like a credit calculator to see how many credits to a class and its pre-reqs, or a multi year scheduler for courses. As well as see progress/track for CIS minors. A possible editable database of courses in case their names or course code changes, or new classes are added (other columns can help narrow down its placement).
